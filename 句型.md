- 定语是一种句子中的成分,基本是形容词

## 主语

动作的发出者,放在**谓语**动词之前的**成分**就是主语

1. **I** love you
2. **Learning EngLish** is easy
3. **She** came to this city to find a job.
4. **The flower** smells great.
5. **That we are best friends** is the happiest thing in this world.

## 谓语

动词就是谓语

1. I **love** you.
2. Learning English **is** easy.
3. She **came to** this city to find a job.
4. The flower **smells** great.
5. That we are best friends **is** the happiest thing in this world.

## 宾语

动作的承受着,放在**实义动词**之后的成分.

*放在系动词之后的不是宾语*



1. I love **you**.
2. Learning English is easy.
3. She came to this **city** to find a **job**.
4. The flower smells great.
5. That we are best friends is the happiest thing in this world.

## 表语

放在系动词之后的**成分**就是表语



1. I love you.
2. Learning English is **easy**. (系动词之后的成分即为表语)
3. She came to this city to find a job.
4. The flower smells **great**. (感官动词是系动词的一种)
5. That we are best **friends** is the happiest **thing** in this world. (are 和 is 之后的都是表语)

## 定语

修饰**名词**或**代词**的**成分**就是**定语**

定语和形容词非常接近(概念上)



1. I love you.

2. Learning English is easy.

3. She came to **this** city to find **a** job. (this修饰城市,a修饰job,限定的定语)

4. The **beautiful** flower smells great. (显然形容词作定语)

5. That we are **best** friends is **the happiest** thing **in this world**. (best修饰friend,happiest修饰thing,in this world 限定)

   

## 状语

只能修饰**除了名词和代词**以外的词的成分就是状语



1. I love you **deeply**.(deeply修饰**爱**)
2. Learning English is easy. 
3. She came to this city **to find a job**. (修饰来城市的目的,本质上是修饰came)
4. The beautiful flower smells **very** great.
5. That we are best friends is the happiest thing in this world.



## 宾语补足语

补充说明**宾语**的成分

> 在疑似为宾语的部分之间加一个系动词,如果句子仍然通顺,就可以判断为宾语补足语



1. I found you **beautiful**.
2. I consider him a good **student**.(补足后面那句话)
3. I hear her **singing**.



## 同位语

进一步**解释说明**前面的名词或代词的成分是同位语

> 可以认为是加深效果
>
> 双宾语和同位语的区别:
>
> ​	A.双宾语(功能互补,缺一不可) : I told them my idea, 我给你十块钱(你和十块钱都必须存在)
>
> ​	B.同位语(功能相同,可以删去其中一个): My friend Jason is awesome.
>
> 双宾语和宾语补足语的区别:
>
> ​	A.双宾语: I offered him my help.
>
> ​	B.宾语补足语: I found him interesting.



1. We **two** will go shopping this afternoon.
2. Her job, **looking after these children**,is difficult.
3. I had no idea **that you were here**.(补充*前面的成分*)



> 形式主语:
>
> 1.定义:为避免头重脚轻,将真正的主语置后,用it做形式上的主语
>
> 2.举例:
>
> - 原句: Getting up at 6am is a good habit.
> - 修改: It is a good habit to get up 6am.
>
> 形式宾语:
>
> 1.将真正的宾语放在后面,用it做形式上的宾语
>
> - 原句: I found getting up at 6am interesting.
> - 改后:I found it interesting to get up at 6am.
